<h2>Privacy Policy for Simple Chatbot</h2>

<p>At Simple Chatbot, the privacy of our users is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that are received and collected by Simple Chatbot and how they are used.</p>

<h3>What do we collect?</h3>

<p>We do not collect any personal information while you interact with the Simple Chatbot.</p>

<h3>Your conversation history</h3>

<p>Your conversation history is temporarily stored for the duration of each session to facilitate the ongoing conversation. This information is deleted at the end of each session and is not stored or used for any other purpose.</p>

<h3>Your privacy is important to us.</h3>

<p>Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We adhere strictly to privacy policies to ensure the information provided by the user is safe and not misused.</p>

<h3>Policy on Sharing of Information</h3>

<p>Simple Chatbot does not share any personal information with third parties.</p>

<h3>Data sent to OpenAI</h3>

<p>As we make use of OpenAI's GPT-4, we must send them the text of your questions. This data is governed by their privacy policy, which can be found at: <a href="https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy">https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy</a>.</p>

<h3>California Residents</h3>

<p>To the extent provided for by law and subject to applicable exceptions, California residents have the following privacy rights in relation to the Personal Information we collect:</p>
<ul>
  <li>The right to know information about our processing of your Personal Information.</li>
  <li>The right to request deletion of your Personal Information.</li>
  <li>The right to correct your Personal Information.</li>
  <li>The right to be free from discrimination relating to the exercise of any of your privacy rights.</li>
</ul>

<p>We do not sell or share Personal Information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). We also do not process sensitive personal information for the purposes of inferring characteristics about a consumer.</p>
